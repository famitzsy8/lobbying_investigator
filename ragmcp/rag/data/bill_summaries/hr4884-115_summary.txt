
     <p><b>Defending Elections from Threats by Establishing Redlines Act of 2018</b></p> <p>This bill requires the Office of the Director of National Intelligence (DNI), no more than 30 days after a U.S. election, to determine whether a foreign government interfered with the election and report on that determination.</p> <p>If the DNI reports that there was not interference and subsequently determines that there was interference, the DNI shall report on the subsequent determination.</p> <p>If the DNI determines that Russia interfered with an election, the President shall impose specified sanctions.</p> <p>The President shall brief Congress on any foreign government the President determines interfered with, or is likely to interfere with, an election.</p>
